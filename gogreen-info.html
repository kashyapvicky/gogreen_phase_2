WHAT IS GO GREEN?

Go Green is a licensed car wash company offering waterless exterior and interior car washing services to many communities in Dubai. In addition, with holding a DED license, Go Green is also pre-approved by your Master Community Management for quality, safety and professionalism.

WHAT ARE THE SERVICES OFFERED BY GO GREEN?

Exterior Wash: For the exterior body of the car from the roof to the rims. We clean all hard surfaces of the car, including the windows and the tires.

Interior Wash: A vacuum treatment for your car, along with junk removal, mats dusting, interior cleaning of all glass surfaces and a sparking dashboard

Engine Detailing: Keep your engine in top condition with our special protective coating that prevents dust accumulation.

HOW CAN YOU WASH A CAR WITHOUT WATER?

Our solution is comprised of a special water-based product containing premium soaps, surfactants and lubricating agents, Carnauba & Montan wax, plus a special polymer polish formula is perfect for old and new cars.

It is wiped away using high-quality microfiber towels. Because no water is needed in our car wash services; there is no mess or toxic run-off.

WON'T WATERLESS CAR WASHING SCRATCH YOUR CAR?

Our method will NOT scratch your car. The innovative formula breaks down, lifts, and encapsulates dirt & grime from the surface of your vehicle. Our hand-washing technique, using plush microfiber towels to wipe away the dirt and buff out the polymers, gives the surface a wax-like finish

WHAT ARE YOUR OPERATING HOURS?

We have different operating hours in different communities, as we need to follow that Master Community’s rules and regulations.

REGISTERING ON THE APP

HOW DO I USE THE GO GREEN APP?

Setup your account in the app. Simply sign up using your email, facebook or google details. Fill in your location details, your car details, and select your package and time when you’d like your car to be washed. 

WHY SHOULD I USE THE GO GREEN APP?

Go Green is convenience at your fingertips! And it saves you time and money. Once registered, you can manage your cars, schedule washes, easily renew your subscriptions, and access your service history, all through our car wash app!

HOW DO I BOOK AN APPOINTMENT?

Once you add your cars under “My Cars” in the app, you will be prompted to select your package through the booking process.

Upon receiving your request for service(s) with desired location, appointment time and successful payment, your schedule service will start. Car was will be done as per the appointment, and you can review the same on your App. You can book modify or add another appointment any time by visiting the “My Cars” page on the app and making the appropriate package selection.

HOW DO I RENEW MY APPOINTMENT?

Automatic renewal notice will appear on your home page, prior to the expiry of your package. Simply click on “RENEW NOW” to renew your package!

HOW MANY DAYS NOTICE IS REQUIRED TO SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT?

You may book an appointment for one-time washes by giving us at least 2 hours’ notice. Monthly packages require 24hrs notice.

CAN I ADD MULTIPLE CARS?

Of course! Simply add your vehicles under “My Cars” and choose which ones you’d like to have washed. Opt for a monthly package to save more on multiple cars.

DO I NEED TO BE WITH MY VEHICLE WHILE IT'S BEING WASHED?
